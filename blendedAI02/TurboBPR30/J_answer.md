# ⚡ TurboBPR 3.0: The Manager’s Quick-Start Guide
**"Re-engineering Government at the Speed of Thought"**

Welcome to the revival of the DoD’s most powerful efficiency engine. You aren't just documenting a process; you are identifying "Bureaucratic Friction" and eliminating it.

## 🚀 Step 1: Define the "AS-IS" (The Current Mess)
Open the **TurboBPR Dashboard**. In the "Process Input" area, list the steps of your current workflow. 
* **Tip from the BPR Expert:** Don’t list what the *manual* says happens. List what *actually* happens—the "hidden" emails, the waiting for signatures, and the "just-in-case" reviews.
* **Format:** Just type them out line-by-line. The **PC-based Expert** ensured you don't need to click 50 menus to add a step.

## 🤖 Step 2: Unleash the AI Auditor
Click **"Run AI Re-Engineering."** Your data is sent to the **Ollama Internal Engine**. It applies the "Hammer/Champy" rules of re-engineering:
* **Outcome over Tasks:** Does this step actually help the citizen?
* **Parallelism:** Why are we doing Step B *after* Step A if they don't depend on each other?
* **The 10x Rule:** The AI will suggest radical deletions, not tiny tweaks.

## 📊 Step 3: Review the "Turbo Delta"
The dashboard will display a comparison table. Look for the **Efficiency Gain**:

$$Efficiency_{Gain} = \frac{Time_{Old} - Time_{New}}{Time_{Old}}$$

If your $Efficiency_{Gain}$ is over **75%**, you have a "Board-Ready" transformation.

## 📄 Step 4: Export Your Evidence
Don't waste time formatting a PowerPoint. 
1.  Click **"Download Word Report"** for a formal DoD-style briefing document.
2.  Click **"Download Markdown"** to instantly share the process map (the Mermaid diagram) with your IT or Dev team.
