TurboBPR 2.5 was a software created by the DoD in the 1990s, as part of the Re-inventing the Government initiative, to consolidate existing BPR tools.

The contents of this directory are an experiment outlined within the book #BlendedAI02.

The experiment was really to show how material that is embedded within the training of major models, converted into patterns, could be elicited by a "nudge".

In this directory, shared the initial prompt that asked, and the main sequence of successive answers from Gemini to refine the concept.

Did not include all the iterations and all the other prompts that added.

This experiement was carried out on 2026-03-25 on Gemini via web, and using only the free tier, in two sections during the day.

As described within the book, received the software with an invitation to share it with others.

Hence, also for this experiment, you are free to develop and evolve and reuse as you see fit- I will do likewise.
